Delete your account

Last updated: June 15, 2026
In-app deletion is available. You can permanently delete your EL Parking account and its associated data directly in the app under Settings → Delete Account.

How to request account deletion

  1. Open EL Parking.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm deletion when prompted (you'll re-authenticate for security).

If you cannot access the app, contact the administrator from the in-app Help section, or via the Support page, and request manual account deletion.

What data is deleted

  • Your user profile — name, email, vehicle details, and registration plate.
  • Your authentication account (Firebase Authentication record).
  • Booking records owned by your account.

What may be retained

  • Security and audit-log entries required for compliance and abuse prevention.
  • Operational backups for a limited retention period, after which they are removed automatically.

Retention period

Primary account data is removed immediately after a successful deletion. Backup retention follows our infrastructure policy and is time-limited.
