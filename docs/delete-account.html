EL Parking — Account Deletion

Last updated: May 5, 2026

You can permanently delete your EL Parking account and associated data directly in the app.

How to request account deletion

  1. Open EL Parking.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm deletion when prompted.

If you cannot access the app, contact the administrator from the in-app Help section and request manual account deletion.

What data is deleted

  • User profile (name, email, vehicle details, plate).
  • User authentication account (Firebase Authentication account).
  • User-owned booking records linked to the account.

What may be retained

  • Security/audit log entries required for compliance and abuse prevention.
  • Operational backups for a limited retention period, then removed automatically.

Retention period

Primary account data is removed immediately after successful deletion. Backup retention follows infrastructure policy and is time-limited.
